Detailed Description

Calculate quadratic equation with complex roots, i.e. b^2 - 4ac < 0.

Author
Renjian-buchai

@description Calculates any quadratic equation in form ax^2 + bx + c.

Quadratic equation: x = (-b +/- sqrt(b^2 - 4ac)) / 2a
